Top 10 Cambodian Esports Teams You Should Know

By Leo Chen | June 20, 2025 | 15 min read

Cambodia's esports scene has experienced explosive growth over the past few years, transforming from a relatively unknown market into a formidable force in Southeast Asian competitive gaming. With the rise of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), PUBG Mobile, and other popular titles, Cambodian teams have consistently proven their mettle on regional and international stages.

This comprehensive guide explores the top 10 Cambodian esports teams that have made significant impacts in the competitive gaming landscape. From dominant MLBB squads to rising PUBG Mobile teams, these organizations represent the best of Cambodia's esports talent and ambition.

The Rise of Cambodian Esports

Cambodia's journey in competitive gaming began gaining momentum around 2018, when mobile gaming started penetrating the Southeast Asian market. The country's young, tech-savvy population quickly embraced esports, with Mobile Legends: Bang Bang becoming particularly popular due to its accessibility and competitive structure.

The establishment of the Mobile Legends Professional League Cambodia (MPL KH) in 2021 marked a turning point, providing a structured platform for local talent to develop and compete. Since then, Cambodian teams have consistently performed well in regional tournaments, often punching above their weight against more established esports nations like Indonesia, the Philippines, and Malaysia.

What makes Cambodian esports unique is the combination of raw talent, strategic thinking, and an unwavering determination to compete at the highest levels. Despite having fewer resources compared to their regional counterparts, Cambodian teams have demonstrated remarkable resilience and innovation in their gameplay.

1. See You Soon (SYS) - The MLBB Powerhouse

See You Soon, commonly known as SYS, stands as Cambodia's most successful Mobile Legends team. Founded in 2020, SYS has dominated the MPL KH scene, winning multiple championships and representing Cambodia in international competitions like the MSC (Mid-Season Cup) and M-World Championship.

The team's success can be attributed to their exceptional macro play and team coordination. SYS players are known for their disciplined approach to the game, executing complex strategies with precision. Their jungler, often considered one of the best in Southeast Asia, has been instrumental in their victories.

In 2024, SYS made history by becoming the first Cambodian team to reach the playoffs of the MSC, defeating teams from more established esports regions. This achievement not only put Cambodia on the map but also inspired a new generation of Cambodian esports athletes.

Key achievements include three consecutive MPL KH championships, multiple regional tournament victories, and consistent top-8 finishes in international competitions. The team's roster has remained relatively stable, allowing for strong team chemistry and strategic development.

2. Burn x Flash (BxF) - The Rising Challengers

Burn x Flash emerged as SYS's primary rivals in the Cambodian MLBB scene. Known for their aggressive playstyle and innovative strategies, BxF has consistently pushed the boundaries of what Cambodian teams can achieve.

The team gained recognition for their unconventional hero picks and bold teamfight executions. Their midlaner, in particular, has gained a reputation for making game-changing plays in high-pressure situations. BxF's approach to the game emphasizes individual skill and mechanical prowess, making them a fan favorite.

While they haven't yet matched SYS's international success, BxF has shown significant improvement in recent seasons. Their second-place finish in MPL KH Season 3 demonstrated their potential, and many analysts believe they're on the cusp of breaking through to the international stage.

BxF's training regimen and commitment to improvement have set new standards for Cambodian esports organizations. They were among the first teams in Cambodia to implement structured coaching, sports psychology, and comprehensive video analysis.

3. Impunity KH - The Veterans

Impunity KH represents the old guard of Cambodian esports, having been active in the scene since 2019. While they may not have the recent championship success of SYS or the flashy playstyle of BxF, Impunity KH brings experience and stability to the competitive landscape.

The team is known for their methodical approach to the game, focusing on objective control and late-game teamfights. Their support player, a veteran of the Cambodian scene, is widely respected for his shot-calling and leadership abilities.

Impunity KH has served as a training ground for many of Cambodia's top players, with several former members going on to join other top teams. This has earned them the nickname "The Academy" among Cambodian esports fans.

Despite facing challenges in recent seasons, Impunity KH remains competitive and continues to contribute to the development of Cambodian esports talent. Their commitment to the scene and willingness to mentor younger players has made them an important part of Cambodia's esports ecosystem.

4. Team Flash Cambodia - The International Ambassadors

Team Flash Cambodia, part of the larger Team Flash organization with branches across Southeast Asia, represents Cambodia's connection to the regional esports network. The team benefits from the resources and expertise of the broader Team Flash brand while maintaining a distinctly Cambodian identity.

Their roster features a mix of experienced players and promising newcomers, creating a balanced team dynamic. Team Flash Cambodia has been particularly successful in PUBG Mobile, where they've achieved notable results in regional competitions.

The team's infrastructure and support systems, backed by the Team Flash organization, have allowed them to compete at a professional level that many purely local teams struggle to match. This has sparked discussions about the role of international organizations in developing local esports scenes.

Team Flash Cambodia's success in multiple game titles demonstrates the versatility of Cambodian esports talent. They've competed in MLBB, PUBG Mobile, and Free Fire, showing that Cambodian players can excel across different competitive gaming genres.

5. MEGA Esports - The Dark Horses

MEGA Esports has consistently been the team that surprises everyone. While they may not always be the favorites, MEGA has a knack for pulling off upsets and performing well when it matters most.

The team's playstyle is characterized by unpredictability and adaptability. They're known for their ability to adjust their strategies mid-series, making them difficult opponents to prepare for. This flexibility has served them well in tournament settings where teams must adapt quickly.

MEGA Esports gained significant attention during MPL KH Season 2, where they made an unexpected run to the finals. Their performance in that tournament showcased the depth of talent in the Cambodian scene and proved that any team could compete at the highest level on their day.

The organization has also been active in developing grassroots esports in Cambodia, organizing local tournaments and providing opportunities for amateur players to compete. This community-focused approach has earned them a loyal fanbase.

6. Resurgence - The Comeback Story

Resurgence lives up to its name, having made several comebacks throughout its history. The team has experienced periods of dominance followed by rebuilding phases, demonstrating resilience and adaptability.

Their most recent resurgence came in 2024, when they assembled a new roster that quickly found success. The team's chemistry and communication improved dramatically, leading to better results in competitive play.

Resurgence is known for their strong individual players, particularly their marksman, who has been recognized as one of Cambodia's best. The team's strategy often revolves around creating opportunities for their star players to shine.

The organization has invested heavily in player development and infrastructure, recognizing that long-term success requires more than just talented players. Their approach to team building and player welfare has set an example for other Cambodian esports organizations.

7. EVOS Cambodia - The Brand Power

EVOS Cambodia represents the entry of major international esports brands into the Cambodian market. As part of the EVOS Esports organization, which has teams across Southeast Asia, EVOS Cambodia brings professional infrastructure and brand recognition to the local scene.

The team has been competitive in both MLBB and PUBG Mobile, leveraging the resources and expertise of the broader EVOS organization. Their players benefit from access to advanced training facilities, sports science support, and exposure to international competition.

EVOS Cambodia's presence in the market has raised the overall level of competition, pushing other teams to improve their infrastructure and professionalism. This has been beneficial for the development of Cambodian esports as a whole.

The team's social media presence and content creation have also helped grow the Cambodian esports audience, bringing more attention and sponsorship opportunities to the scene.

8. AURA Esports Cambodia - The Strategic Masters

AURA Esports Cambodia has built a reputation for their strategic depth and analytical approach to the game. The team places a strong emphasis on preparation and game knowledge, often surprising opponents with well-executed strategies.

Their coach, a former professional player, has been instrumental in developing the team's strategic identity. AURA is known for their innovative draft strategies and ability to counter popular meta compositions.

While they may not always have the flashiest players, AURA's success comes from superior teamwork and execution. They've proven that strategic thinking and coordination can overcome individual skill advantages.

The team has been particularly successful in best-of-series formats, where their preparation and adaptability give them an edge. Their performance in playoff scenarios has earned them respect throughout the Cambodian esports community.

9. ONIC Esports Cambodia - The Rising Stars

ONIC Esports Cambodia, part of the renowned ONIC organization, represents another major brand's investment in Cambodian esports. While they're relatively new to the scene compared to some other teams, ONIC Cambodia has quickly established themselves as contenders.

The team benefits from ONIC's proven track record in Indonesia, where ONIC Esports has been one of the most successful MLBB teams. This expertise and knowledge transfer have accelerated ONIC Cambodia's development.

Their roster features a mix of local talent and players with international experience, creating a unique team dynamic. ONIC Cambodia's aggressive playstyle and mechanical skill have made them exciting to watch.

The organization's commitment to developing Cambodian talent while maintaining high competitive standards has made them an important part of the local esports ecosystem. Their presence has also helped attract more investment and attention to Cambodian esports.

10. Geek Fam Cambodia - The Community Builders

Geek Fam Cambodia rounds out our top 10, representing an organization that has been instrumental in building the Cambodian esports community. Beyond competitive success, Geek Fam has focused on grassroots development and community engagement.

The team has been competitive in multiple game titles, showing versatility and adaptability. Their PUBG Mobile squad, in particular, has achieved notable results in regional competitions.

Geek Fam Cambodia's commitment to community development includes organizing tournaments, providing coaching for amateur players, and creating content that educates and entertains the Cambodian esports audience.

Their approach to esports goes beyond just winning games; they're building a sustainable ecosystem that can support long-term growth. This community-focused philosophy has earned them respect and loyalty from Cambodian esports fans.

The Future of Cambodian Esports

As we look toward the future, Cambodian esports appears poised for continued growth and success. The teams listed above represent just the beginning of what's possible for Cambodian competitive gaming.

Several factors are contributing to this positive outlook. First, the increasing investment from international organizations brings resources and expertise that can elevate the entire scene. Second, the success of teams like SYS on the international stage has proven that Cambodian players can compete with the best in the world.

Third, the growing infrastructure and support systems, including better training facilities, coaching, and player development programs, are creating a more professional environment. Finally, the passionate fanbase and community support provide the foundation for sustainable growth.

However, challenges remain. Cambodian teams still face resource constraints compared to their regional competitors. Sponsorship opportunities, while growing, are not yet at the level seen in more established esports markets. Additionally, the need for better infrastructure and support systems continues to be a priority.

Despite these challenges, the trajectory of Cambodian esports is undeniably positive. The teams we've highlighted in this article have shown that with talent, dedication, and the right support, Cambodian esports can compete at the highest levels.

Conclusion

Cambodia's esports scene has come a long way in a relatively short time. From humble beginnings to international recognition, the journey has been remarkable. The top 10 teams we've explored represent the best of Cambodian competitive gaming, each contributing in their own way to the growth and development of the scene.

Whether it's SYS's dominance, BxF's innovation, Impunity's experience, or the community-building efforts of teams like Geek Fam, each organization plays a vital role in Cambodia's esports ecosystem. As the scene continues to evolve, these teams will likely face new challenges and opportunities.

For fans of competitive gaming, Cambodian esports offers exciting storylines, talented players, and the thrill of watching an underdog region compete with established powerhouses. For aspiring players, the success of these teams demonstrates that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible.

As we continue to follow the growth of Cambodian esports, one thing is clear: this is just the beginning. The future looks bright, and these top 10 teams are leading the way.
