Sport Detail: Olympic Games 2024 - New Records Set

By Alex Turner | July 15, 2024

The 2024 Olympic Games in Paris have been a showcase of athletic excellence, with new world records set in track and field events. Athletes from over 100 countries competed, inspiring millions of fans worldwide.

One of the highlights was the men's 100m final, where a new world record was set by American sprinter John Davis, clocking in at 9.57 seconds. In the women's marathon, Kenyan runner Mary Njeri broke the previous record by over a minute.

Beyond the records, the Games have been a celebration of unity and sportsmanship. The opening ceremony featured a stunning display of French culture and history, and athletes have praised the organization and facilities.
